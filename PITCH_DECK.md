## Presentation Notes

- Use smooth transitions between slides
- Pause after "The Problem" slide for impact
- Demo live platform on "Demo Highlights" slide
- Show code/architecture on "Technical Achievements"

### Key Talking Points
1. **Problem**: Emphasize the scale and urgency
2. **Solution**: Highlight anonymity and safety
3. **Features**: Focus on what makes it unique
4. **Technology**: Show technical competence
5. **Impact**: Connect to real-world change
6. **Future**: Show vision and scalability

2. Submit a test story (anonymous)
3. Show story feed and filtering
4. Demonstrate support reactions

6. Explain chat functionality
